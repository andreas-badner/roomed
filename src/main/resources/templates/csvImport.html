Please select a csv file and note that certain formats must be adhered to!

It is implied that there is a header in the submitted file.

Rooms:

Location; name; maximum number of persons; equipment; telephone; notes; office; mail address

The equipment items shall be separated by comma.

User:

Firstname; lastname; mail adresss

Meetings:

title; startdate; enddate; participants; owner; confirmed; description

The equipment items and participants shall be separated by comma. A participant has to have mail adress, firstname and lastname, separated by _ . The dates should be in the LocalDateTime format.
